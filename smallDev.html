Frequently asked questions

I cannot see all my accounts. Where can I find them?

In Easy banking, you will only see accounts with the following features:

  • Only individual current and savings accounts of which you are the holder or a proxy. You can still view all other accounts such as term deposits, professional accounts etc. in your current PC banking.

Any accounts you have hidden in PC banking will also be hidden in Easy banking. If you would like to change this, you can do so via "Your preferences" > "Hide accounts" in PC banking.
